About Me

My passion for software development began in high school when I enrolled in my first Computer Science course. I was exposed to the wonders and versatilities of Java and even though the course was really fundamental, I knew I had found my passion.

I started off college at University of California, San Diego as a Computer Science major, but life caught up to me and a long series of events led me to University of California, Irvine as a Criminology, Law, and Society major. For the previous 2 years, computer Science had always been in the back of my mind, but the major was officially impacted and it was nearly impossible to pursue a change of major.

Even still, I was determined to study computer science and pursue a career in software development. I took all the classes I could register and was fortunately selected for an position to work on a project called Eleveight. Web and mobile app development at Eleveight opened the door to many opportunities and was finally my first step into the tech world.
